I. Storage of Data

In this section, information is given about the methods that we use and do not use under the name of data storage.

a. Data storage on the server

Our application does not store your data on the server. All your data is stored on your device.

b. Cookies

Our application does not use cookies to store your data. However, the google translate feature in our application stores your choice in cookies so that you do not translate the language you translate again every time.

c. Indexed DB

Our application does not use IndexedDB technology used to store data.

d. Local Storage

Our application stores your shopping list in the local warehouse. Thus, when you close and open our application, your shopping list is prevented from being deleted.

e. Session Storage

Our application does not use session storage technology used to store data.

II. Sharing of Data

Our application does not share your data with third-party institutions or organizations.
